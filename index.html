What is R-S-M-T?

For multiple decades, tutoring has been essential to nurture the next generation of mathematicians. We are a team of students who want to make tutoring accessible to everyone. Websites have the possibility to make tutoring easier and better than before, with many tools and features that can aid tutors. This is R-S-M-T, a simple showcase of the capable nature of websites in this industry.
